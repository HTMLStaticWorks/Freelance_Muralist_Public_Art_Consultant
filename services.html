Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commissioning a mural.

How long does a mural take?

Timelines vary greatly based on size and complexity. An interior 10x10ft wall usually takes 2-4 days, while a large exterior facade can take 2-4 weeks. We provide a detailed schedule during the proposal phase.

Do you provide the materials?

Yes, all paints, equipment, scaffolding, and protective coatings are included in the project quote. We use high-grade, industrial-strength paints specifically chosen for the surface and location of your mural.

What if I don't have a design?

That's what we're here for! Our consultation process is designed to extract your brand values or personal aesthetic to create a completely custom concept from scratch. We provide digital mockups for approval before painting starts.

Do you work outside of NYC?

Absolutely. While we are based in New York, we frequently travel for residential and commercial commissions worldwide. Travel and lodging costs are handled transparently as part of the project agreement.
